Apoctober Creatures

Coven

I tried to play with lighting with this one. Having the light of the cauldron cover the three witches that surround it. As for the witches themselves, I tried to invoke a sense of just stumbling upon this in the middle of the jungle, far away from society.

Decapod

At first I had a mental block with this prompt. As much as crustacians appeal to me I had a mental block. I then had the idea of making it an artifact rather than a creature itself. Perhaps something that wasn't a creature itself but had the means to acess one.

Glutton

For this one I wanted to stray away from the typical depictions of gluttony, a large bulbous creature. So insead I tried to make something a little more frail looking but upon closer inspection was something ravenous.

Spine

Going into this one I knew that I wanted a massive spinosaurous looking spine. However, when I got to the body I couldn't think of what to do. What I ended up doing was making it look like some sort of mutation from "The Thing" and I think that it turned out really well. It is just concise enough to kind of look like a creature while still looking like a strange hybrid creature.

Titan

I wanted to make a giant manta ray looking thing that carried mountains on its giant back. I also wanted to make it look like it was a gentle giant carrying a colony of some sort within its mountains.

Undead

I wanted to make a more humanoid undead creature but I still wanted to make it deformed in some way. So what I decided to do was a mummy-like creature with its body bisected straight down the middle.
