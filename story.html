"The Snow" Storyboards

Scene 1

A man walks out of his home and notices that he does not have enough firewood. With a worried look he prepares himself to venture into the woods as the camera pans towards the moon.

Scene 2

The camera pans down from the moon to a clearing where the man is chopping wood. He picks up his bag and starts to walk back but accidentally slips. He begins to fall asleep in the snow.

Scene 3

The man is woken up by a cowgirl on an alien planet. He walks with her confused as to what is happening. He then gets startled and turns around.

Scene 4

As he turns around, he now finds himself in a neverending hallway. He walks down the halway and continuously picks up speed as doors open up with various scenery behind them. He slows down and walks towards the camera.

Scene 5

The man finds himself in a colorful street. A street vendor beckons him over and tries to sell him something. The vendor then points to the sky and runs away. The man turns around and sees the moon falling.

Scene 6

The man finds himself back in the field, laying in the snow. He gets up and walks off screen. A door closes and it is revealed he is still in the hallway.
